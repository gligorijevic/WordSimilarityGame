In order to play the game, please go to page. There below the welcome message you will see two words or phrases. Words that are chosen are properties of two parallel ontologies, and represent some relation between two objects (there is large variety of objects in ontologies). Right below them, you will see two links, each leading to the description of word above it. Be careful, words may appear very similar and present two very different things, and other way around. You will be asked to rate similarity between a pair of words using star icons.

There are ten stars and a button to clear all selection. The rules of this game are simple:

  • If you don't select anything your grade of similarity between two words is zero which means that you think the two words are not similar at all. On the other hand, if you grade similarity between two words with ten start, that means you belive the words are similar. When you click on stars to rate, you will receive notification of grade you have chosen.
  • Clicking on button "Rate and next" you will get new pair of words to rate. Repeat process until you want to and click "Finish playing" to save your results.

At any time, you can preview your previous results by clicking on link in your menu. There you can edit your previous grades if you feel you had made a mistake during playing the game.
